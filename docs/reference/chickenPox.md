# Hospital admissions by chicken pox in Brazil

Monthly hospital admissions by chicken pox in Brazil from January 2010
to December 2019.

## Usage

``` r
chickenPox
```

## Format

A data frame with 120 rows and 6 columns:

- date:

  The date of the observations.

- \< 5 year, 5 to 9 years, 10 to 14 years, 15 to 49 years, 50 years or
  more:

  The number of admissions for each age group.
